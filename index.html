About Me

Hi! My name is Wasim Haider and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in User Experience and User Interface design started back in 2015, when I decided to continue working as UI/UX designer, I started enjoying working on that. With the time i started understanding the User Experience a bit deeply.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at a tech giant, and a start-up, My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Edkasa for a noble cause of spreading education.
